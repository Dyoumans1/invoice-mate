About InvoiceMaid

InvoiceMaid is a simple app where you can make and keep track of all your invoices. Perfect for freelancers and small businesses who need an easy way to manage their billing.

Features

  • Create and save client information
  • Generate professional invoices
  • Track payment status
  • Simple and easy to use interface
